April 8, 2017

BLOOD DONATION CAMP

A II Blood Donation Camp of the session was successfully held and organised in the Examination Hall, NIT Kurukshetra. The camp was conducted by the Red Cross Society, Delhi. The Camp was organised at a much larger scale with a 50% hike in the capacity. We would sincerely like to thank all NIT-KKR students for their continued support in our endeavours for a brighter future for all! We also extend are heartiest thanx to the Alumni Association for both their financial and moral support.


January 21, 2017

MAKE A WISH

This Techspardha'17, Rooh and Shiksha continued their collaboration and launched a Make A Wish campaign that saw people of NIT Kurukshetra show their love for the little ones and contribute in fulfilling their wishes. The campaign ended on the penultimate day of the annual techno-managerial fest. The campaign saw a fruitfull result with wishes of over 50 children being fulfilled. The donations were accepted both in cash and kind.


November 19, 2016

BLOOD DONATION CAMP

The I Blood Donation Camp of the session was successfully held and organised in the Examination Hall, NIT Kurukshetra. The camp was conducted by the Red Cross Society, Rohtak. A huge response saw people being turned back due to limited units. The camp can be stated as one of the most successfull ever in the history of the Institute with over 250 units being collected within 5 hours. We are proud of our fellow students of NIT Kurukshetra and all others who made it a grand success.

CHANGE OF SPONSORS

HOXGENE:BUY WITH US

In our quest to improve and facilitate the education of more and more lesser priveledged children, we need to constantly improve ourselves. DATABASE COLLECTION

In building a closer and a more inclusive student family in our campus, we have taken a new step that aims at providing a list of potential donors that are willing to donate their blood in case of an emergency to anyone related to the Institute or even maybe from the city.
